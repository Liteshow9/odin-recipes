Fav Eggs

Description

I absolutely love eggs, from my head down to my legs.

This recipe is the perfect way to wake up in the morning. I'm excited to share it with you and I hope that you like it!

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1 Slice Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Large White Mushroom
  • 1 Tbsp Milk
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs and milk together in a bowl
  2. Break the cheese slice into small pieces and chop the mushroom into pieces, then add to the bowl of egg.
  3. Add a pinch of tsp of salt, pepper, and garlic powder to your mix.
  4. Apply the butter to a pre-heated frying pan over medium heat. Spread evenly to create a non-stick surface.
  5. Add the eggs mix to the pan and monitor it closely. As the underside of the egg begins to solify, begin to flip and scramble the eggs until fully cooked.
  6. Serve on a plate and enjoy!
